We will be hosting the event at the lovely Hilton Resort & Spa in sunny San Diego.

We have reserved a block of rooms at a special rate of $179/night + tax. The discounted rate will be available until January 9th, or until the block sells out, whichever comes first. To book your room please click here or call 619-276-4010 and mention the Group Code CIP118.

Daytime parking is self-parking. Please click here for a map of the daytime parking lot. Daytime parking rates: $5 up to 3 hours, $8 for 3-6 hours, $12 for 6-18 hours.

We have a discounted overnight parking rate of $20/night. All parking is self-parking and located throughout the Resort. At check-in, guests will receive a parking gate key and a pass to put in their cars.
